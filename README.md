# Project: Keshav Water Industries (KWI) - Premium Web Experience
**Reference Aesthetic:** https://wholesomeharvestglobal.com/
**Tone:** Industrial Luxury, Clinical Precision, Organic Minimalism.

## 1. Visual Identity & Design Tokens
- **Typography:** 
  - Headings: 'Playfair Display' or 'Bormioli' (Sophisticated Serif).
  - Body: 'Inter' or 'Plus Jakarta Sans' (Clean Sans-Serif, 0.05em letter spacing).
- **Color Palette:**
  - Primary: `#001B3D` (Deep Midnight Navy)
  - Secondary: `#0077B6` (Liquid Blue)
  - Accent: `#F8F9FA` (Soft Porcelain White for background breathing room)
  - Text: `#1A1A1A` (Charcoal for readability)
- **Border Radius:** Extreme organic rounding (`40px` to `60px` for containers).
- **Layout:** Asymmetrical grids. Avoid standard 3-column rows. Use overlapping "Bento" style boxes and large hero text.

## 2. Business Logic & Authority
- **Legal Entity:** Keshav Water Industries (Est. 2011).
- **Certifications:** UDYAM-DL-08-0031761 | GSTIN: 07ATHPK7730P1Z3.
- **Core Specialization:** High-capacity water treatment for Medical (Dialysis) and Industrial (ETP/STP) sectors.

- `/` (Home): The "Hero" experience.
